The World of Anime

What is Anime

Anime is a style of animation popular in Japanese films and television series. It often combines stark, colorful graphics with action-packed plots. Early anime films were intended primarily for a Japanese audience. Therefore, they used many cultural references unique to Japan It contains various genres and tend to be very captivative
